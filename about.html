About LogiStream

LogiStream is a logistics project built around clear service, simple communication, and dependable delivery support in Cambodia.

The goal is to help customers understand their shipping options, check shipment progress, and reach support quickly when they need help.

What We Stand For

Clear Communication

We use simple language so customers know what to do and what to expect.

Reliable Service

We focus on careful delivery handling and practical support for every shipment.

Helpful Support

When customers need help, we want the next step to be easy to follow.
